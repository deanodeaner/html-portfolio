The Best Movies According to Dean

My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers: Endgame

The best conclusion to a franchise! Or maybe a Marvel saga...

  • Special mention to my favourite Marvel characters: Doctor Strange and Scarlet Witch!

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Yes, another Marvel movie! This brought some nostalgic vibe when the previous Spider-Men showed up. Toby's Spider-Man brings back awesome childhood memories!

Free Guy

I'm a fan of Deadpool and Steve Harrington so I was eager to watch this film! This has programming involved, I see this film as one of my motivation to learn web development (and CS in general)!
